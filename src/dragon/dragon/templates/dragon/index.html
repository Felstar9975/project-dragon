Welcome to UniGames

Unigames is the table-top gaming club at the University of Western Australia. We do roleplaying, wargaming, board and card gaming and indeed gaming in all forms (except video games, try next door at UCC for that). Whether it's D&D, Magic the Gathering, Warhammer 40K, or any of our dozens of other systems and games, we've got you covered. We also have some chess sets, Mahjong, and at least 2 sets of Monopoly just in case. Check out our Library.

Membership costs $5 per year for Guild Members (but $7 for everyone else) and entitles you to vote at the annual committee elections, join one of our many long-running campaigns, borrow our books and games, and receive a 10% discount when you buy from our generous sponsor Tactics. We also keep a well stocked fidge in our clubroom for all your mid-game snacking needs. Our snacks are some of the cheapest on campus!

The clubroom is currently located on the top level of Cameron Hall, which is above the Tavern. Ask for directions at the Guild Student Centre (ground floor in Guild Village).

A good place to start looking for information is the Frequently Asked Questions. Otherwise, pick something interesting from the menu on the right!
